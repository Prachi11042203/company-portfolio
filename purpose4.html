spiralmon Launches Its New Brand Purpose Statement

15 Jun 2021
spiralmoon Technologies, the fastest growing next-generation provider of IT and consulting services has rolled-out a new purpose statement – ‘Creating smiles through great people and technology’.
With an ever-expanding portfolio of capabilities, it was time for SpiralMoon to delve deep and identify the source of its motivation.
Although technology is at the core of its solutions, it is still the people and their passion that fuel spi’s commitment towards creating smiles.
Expressing his thoughts on the new purpose statement,
R Srikrishna – CEO, spiralmoon Technologies said, “21K SpiralMoon will do their best every day to bring you, your colleagues and your customers a smile. Here is our new purpose.”
